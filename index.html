Overview

The Equally Effective Alternate Access Plan (EEAAP) addresses the what, why, how, and when of what we do to provide equal opportunities for ANY student to be able to learn.

This guide is divided into three main sections. First, above and at the top, are links to official organizations that provide guidance for what we do and how we do it. Next, and to the left, are some general introductions into the concepts behind the EEAAP. And finally, below, are links to individual departments with resources and recommendations for each of them. Each department contains the following pages to help provide ideas.
