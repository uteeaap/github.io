    Excerpt: "The midimag-ex list is an international forum for sight-impaired musicians, producers, and sound engineers of all levels, from hobbyist to advanced professional. Anything related to this broad topic area is acceptable for discussion, though because of the nature of the list, accessibility topics arise frequently.
