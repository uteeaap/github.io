The Special Challenge of Music

Music has a special challenge not found in most other fields. One must be a consumer and producer simulatneously. In other words, a musician must be a consumer ("reading" notes) as well as a producer ("play music") at the same time. Most solutions in accessibility exist because we can allow someone to be a consumer in one format, but a producer in another. This isn't true for music, and finding ways for musicians to learn in a manner without significant time penalties is a challenge.
